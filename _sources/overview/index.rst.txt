BirdSongToolbox is an open-source Python module for processing, analysis, and
visualization of neural and behavioral data. Its goal is to make flexible
analysis of sequential motor behavior easier. Pages describing the overall
design philosophy of BirdSongToolbox, background information of birdsong
behavior, and data structures used by BirdSongToolbox. installation procedure
are listed below.

The
:ref:`Glossary <glossary>` has definitions for many key terms that are
helpful to understand when using BirdSongToolbox to do analysis.
